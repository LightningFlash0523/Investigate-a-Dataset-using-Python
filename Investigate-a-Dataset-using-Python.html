Question of Analysis¶

The objective of this study is to determine the factors that have affected the survivorship of the passengers in the titanic incident. Specifically, if gender, passenger class which describes the socio-economic status, age, port of embarkation, being with someone or alone during the incident have affected their survival chances.

    Test of Independence¶

    This part tested the independence of each identified factors, Sex, Pclass, Age - Child or Not, Being alone or with someone, and the Embarked factor to the survivorship of the passengers.

    Testing Sex Factor

    Ho: Gender does not significantly affect the survivorship of a passenger in the titanic incident.

    Ha: Gender significantly affects the survivorship of a passenger in the titanic incident.

    Since our dependent variable is categorical, I applied chi-square independence test.

    def chi_square_test(ind_var, dep_var, dataset):
    pivot = pd.pivot_table(data = dataset[[dep_var, ind_var]], index = dep_var, columns = [ind_var], aggfunc = len)
    print(pivot)
    print ("\n")
    chi2, p_value, dof, expected = chi2_contingency(pivot)
    print ("Results of Chi-Squared test on {} to {}.".format(ind_var,dep_var))
    print ("Chi-Squared Score = " + str(chi2))
    print ("Pvalue = " + str(p_value))

    Summary¶

    From what we have seen in our charts and visual, and upon verification thru hypothesis testing, we can now say that the factors that affect the survivorship of the passengers in the titanic incident were:

  • Gender - female had higher survivorship than male;

  • Pclass - those who were in a higher socio-economic status had higher survivorship than those in the lower socio-economic status;

  • Age - Children had higher survivorship than adult;

  • Embarked - those who embarked at Cherbourg had higher survivorship than the others; and

  • those who were with someone had higher survivorship than those who were alone in the titanic incident.

    • Limitations¶

  • Since the dataset had a lot of missing values, the remedy applied, which is simply dropping of null values, might not be the best of option for this study. We can test for different ways to handle this limitation such as filling up the missing with the average age value, or apply machine learning algorithm.

  • Please also note that what we have found in this study are purely correlations of the different factors, and correlation does not imply causation.
